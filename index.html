Our Story

We needed it, so we built it.

Existing field-service platforms cover a wide range of industries and use cases. That breadth is a strength for them — and a real problem for operators who just need to run appliance repair companies without buying into a system designed for HVAC, plumbing, and pest control at the same time.

The pricing reflects that breadth too. Monthly costs scale fast. Features get locked behind tiers. Simple operations end up paying for complexity they will never use.

We built S-Protocol because we needed a system that fit how we actually work. It covers what matters for appliance repair — jobs, customers, technicians, workflow — and nothing else. Now we are making it available to other operators who want the same thing.
