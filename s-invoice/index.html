Who it’s for

Built for independent service pros and small crews.

If your work happens at the customer’s door — quotes, jobs, and getting paid — S Invoice keeps the paperwork simple. It’s made for businesses like:

What you get

The paperwork side of the job, handled.

Everything you need to quote work, bill for it, and keep clean records — nothing you don’t.

Professional invoices

Clean, itemized invoices with your logo, brand color, notes, and tax settings — shareable with customers as polished invoice links.

Estimates

Quote the work first. Build itemized estimates, send them to the customer, and convert an approved estimate into an invoice in one step.

Customer records

Keep every customer’s contact details, documents, and billing history in one place — so repeat work starts from what you already know.

Payment tracking

Record payments against every invoice — including partial payments and refunds — and always know what’s outstanding.

Online payments with Stripe

Connect Stripe to let customers pay invoices online by card. Payments reconcile automatically against the invoice.

Photos & attachments

Attach photos and documents to invoices — before-and-after shots, receipts, and paperwork stay with the record they belong to.

How it works

Estimate. Invoice. Get paid.

A workflow that matches how service work actually happens — from your phone’s browser at the job site or from a laptop at home.

Send the estimate

Price the job with itemized line items and share it with your customer before the work starts.

Turn it into an invoice

When the work is approved or done, convert the estimate straight into an invoice — no retyping.

Track the payment

Collect online through Stripe or record cash and checks. Partial payments and refunds are handled too.

Work from anywhere

Mobile-first, wherever the job takes you.

S Invoice is a web app designed for small screens first — create an estimate in the driveway, convert it in the kitchen, and send the invoice before you leave. No installation required: it runs in the browser on your phone, tablet, or computer.
